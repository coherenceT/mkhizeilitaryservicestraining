Training Pathway

Your complete journey from civilian to military excellence. Follow our proven 3-step pathway designed to maximize your potential and ensure success.

YOUR JOURNEY

The Complete Military Training Pathway

A structured progression from application to active military service

1

Application & Assessment

Complete comprehensive application process with fitness testing, medical evaluation, and personal interviews to determine your readiness and commitment.

4-6 weeks

Requirements:

  • Online application form
  • Fitness assessment (2.4km run, push-ups, sit-ups)
  • Medical examination
  • Psychological evaluation
  • Personal interview

Outcomes:

  • Acceptance or conditional acceptance
  • Training camp assignment
  • Preparation guidelines
  • Mentorship assignment
2

Pre-Military Training

Intensive 12-week residential programme building physical fitness, mental resilience, leadership skills, and military fundamentals.

12 weeks

Requirements:

  • Full-time residential commitment
  • Medical fitness clearance
  • Personal equipment (provided)
  • Disciplinary standards compliance
  • Academic prerequisites met

Outcomes:

  • Military fitness certification
  • Leadership competency assessment
  • Basic military skills qualification
  • Character development evaluation
  • Career guidance and placement
3

Military Service

Full military training and active service in your chosen branch with specialized tracks and career development opportunities.

2+ years

Requirements:

  • Successful programme completion
  • Security clearance
  • Branch-specific requirements
  • Ongoing fitness maintenance
  • Professional conduct standards

Outcomes:

  • Full military rank and status
  • Specialized training completion
  • Career advancement opportunities
  • Leadership positions
  • Civilian career transition support

WEEK-BY-WEEK

12-Week Training Timeline

Detailed breakdown of your transformation journey

Week 1-2

Foundation Building

Establish baseline fitness, introduce military discipline, and build team cohesion through foundational training.

Physical Training
  • Fitness assessment and baseline establishment
  • Basic running and conditioning drills
  • Introduction to military physical training
Military Fundamentals
  • Military bearing and discipline
  • Basic commands and protocols
  • Uniform and equipment familiarization
Week 3-4

Strength Development

Focus on building physical strength, endurance, and mental resilience through progressive training protocols.

Advanced Conditioning
  • Strength training circuits
  • Endurance building programmes
  • Obstacle course introduction
Mental Training
  • Stress management techniques
  • Goal setting and achievement
  • Overcoming physical discomfort
Week 5-6

Skills Acquisition

Introduction to essential military skills including navigation, field craft, and basic combat techniques.

Field Craft
  • Map reading and navigation
  • Camouflage and concealment
  • Movement techniques
Team Skills
  • Team building exercises
  • Communication protocols
  • Leadership opportunities
Week 7-8

Survival Training

Essential wilderness survival skills and adaptation to challenging environments.

Survival Skills
  • Shelter construction
  • Fire starting techniques
  • Water purification
  • Food procurement
Environmental Adaptation
  • Different terrain navigation
  • Weather condition adaptation
  • Resource management
Week 9-10

Leadership Development

Advanced leadership training with practical application and command responsibilities.

Leadership Skills
  • Command and control principles
  • Decision-making under pressure
  • Team motivation techniques
Practical Application
  • Squad leadership exercises
  • Training coordination
  • Performance evaluation
Week 11-12

Final Assessment & Graduation

Comprehensive evaluation of all skills and preparation for military service transition.

Final Assessments
  • Physical fitness testing
  • Skills proficiency evaluation
  • Leadership assessment
  • Character evaluation
Transition Preparation
  • Career guidance
  • Military branch selection
  • Graduation ceremony
  • Next steps planning
