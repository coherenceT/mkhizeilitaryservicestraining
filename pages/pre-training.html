TRAINING MODULES

Core Curriculum Components

Comprehensive skill development across all military disciplines

Physical Training Curriculum

Elite physical conditioning programme designed to exceed military fitness standards and prepare candidates for the most demanding physical challenges.

Cardiovascular Excellence

  • Progressive running programmes (5km to 21km)
  • High-intensity interval training
  • Swimming and water-based conditioning
  • Aerobic capacity development
  • Recovery and injury prevention

Strength & Power

  • Functional strength training
  • Olympic weightlifting fundamentals
  • Bodyweight strength progression
  • Power development exercises
  • Muscular endurance building

Combat Fitness

  • Military obstacle courses
  • Close-quarters battle preparation
  • Load-bearing endurance training
  • Tactical movement techniques
  • Functional fitness integration

Discipline & Ethics Training

Comprehensive character development focusing on military values, discipline, and ethical decision-making essential for military service.

Military Discipline

  • Time management and punctuality
  • Attention to detail
  • Following orders and protocols
  • Self-discipline development
  • Accountability and responsibility

Ethical Framework

  • Military code of conduct
  • Ethical decision-making
  • Integrity and honour
  • Service before self
  • Moral courage development

Professional Bearing

  • Military courtesy and respect
  • Personal presentation standards
  • Communication protocols
  • Chain of command understanding
  • Professional conduct

Mental Resilience Modules

Advanced psychological training to develop unbreakable mental fortitude, stress management, and performance under pressure.

Stress Management

  • Breathing and relaxation techniques
  • Stress response recognition
  • Coping strategies development
  • Performance anxiety management
  • Resilience building exercises

Decision Making

  • Rapid decision frameworks
  • Risk assessment training
  • Problem-solving under pressure
  • Critical thinking development
  • Intuitive decision processes

Mental Toughness

  • Overcoming physical discomfort
  • Mental barriers identification
  • Goal setting and achievement
  • Self-motivation techniques
  • Long-term mindset development

Survival Basics

Essential wilderness survival skills and field craft training for operation in diverse environments and challenging conditions.

Wilderness Survival

  • Shelter construction techniques
  • Fire starting and maintenance
  • Water procurement and purification
  • Food foraging and preparation
  • Environmental adaptation

Field Craft Skills

  • Map reading and navigation
  • Camouflage and concealment
  • Movement techniques
  • Observation and reporting
  • Basic tracking skills

Emergency Response

  • Basic first aid and trauma care
  • Emergency signaling
  • Resource management
  • Survival psychology
  • Extraction and rescue procedures
