About the Programme

Discover how our comprehensive pre-military training programme prepares young South Africans for the challenges and honours of national service.

Our Mission & Purpose

The National Military Training Programme serves as a vital bridge between civilian life and military service, providing comprehensive preparation for young South Africans who aspire to serve their country. Our programme goes beyond physical training to develop well-rounded individuals with the discipline, resilience, and leadership qualities essential for military excellence.

Through intensive training and character development, we ensure that candidates are not only physically prepared for the rigours of military service but also mentally and emotionally equipped to handle the responsibilities that come with defending our nation.

REQUIREMENTS

Eligibility & Requirements

Who can join our prestigious programme

Age Requirements

  • 18-22 years old
  • Grade 12 completion or equivalent
  • Medical fitness certificate
  • South African citizenship

Physical Standards

  • 2.4km run under 12 minutes
  • Push-ups: 20+ repetitions
  • Sit-ups: 30+ repetitions
  • Basic medical clearance

Academic Requirements

  • Grade 12 certificate
  • Mathematics literacy or higher
  • English proficiency
  • Clean criminal record

Commitment Requirements

  • Full-time programme participation
  • Residential training camp
  • 12-week minimum commitment
  • Disciplinary standards adherence
